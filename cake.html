Chocolate Cake


Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Baking powder
  • Milk

Procedure:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease your cake pan.
  2. In a bowl, sift together flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add water, oil, vinegar, and vanilla extract to the dry mix and whisk until smooth.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 30–35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  5. Let the cake cool completely before frosting or serving.
