Chocochip Cookies


Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Choco chips
  • Milk
  • Baking powder

Procedure:

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F) and line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. Beat softened butter with white and brown sugar until light and creamy. Add an egg and vanilla extract, and mix well.
  3. In another bowl, combine all-purpose flour, baking soda, and a pinch of salt. Gradually add it to the wet mixture.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips evenly into the dough using a spatula or spoon.
  5. Scoop small portions onto the tray and bake for 10–12 minutes. Let them cool on a wire rack before serving.
