Brownie

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Eggs

Procedure:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and line a square baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, melt butter and chopped chocolate together using a double boiler or microwave. Stir until smooth.
  3. Add sugar, eggs, and vanilla to the melted mixture. Then fold in flour, cocoa powder, and a pinch of salt.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and spread evenly. Bake for 25–30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out with moist crumbs.
  5. Let the brownies cool in the pan, then cut into squares and serve. Enjoy warm or chilled!
