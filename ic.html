Chocolate Icecream

Ingredients:

  • Fresh Cream
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Vannila essence
  • Milk

Procedure:

  1. Beat chilled heavy cream until it forms soft peaks using a whisk or hand mixer.
  2. Gently fold in sweetened condensed milk and your desired flavor (e.g., vanilla, chocolate, mango).
  3. Combine carefully until smooth and creamy without deflating the whipped cream.
  4. Pour the mixture into a freezer-safe container and level the top with a spatula.
