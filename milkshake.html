Chocolate Milkshake

Ingredients:

  • Cocoa powder
  • Sugar
  • Ice cream
  • Ice cube
  • Milk

Procedure:

  1. Decide your milkshake flavor (e.g., chocolate, vanilla, strawberry, or mango) and prepare the main ingredient (like chopped fruit or syrup).
  2. Add 1 cup of cold milk, 2–3 scoops of ice cream, and your chosen flavor ingredient into a blender.
  3. Blend the mixture for 30–60 seconds until smooth and creamy.
  4. Add more milk if it's too thick or more ice cream if it's too thin, then blend again briefly.
  5. Pour into a tall glass, top with whipped cream, nuts, or chocolate syrup, and serve chilled with a straw.
